About monster

"Monster" follows the story of Dr. Kenzou Tenma, a brilliant brain surgeon, whose life takes a dark turn when he makes a fateful decision to save a young boy's life over the city's mayor. As the consequences of his choice unfold, Tenma finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracies, moral dilemmas, and the pursuit of a mysterious and enigmatic serial killer.
